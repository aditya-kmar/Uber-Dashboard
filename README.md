# 🚖 Uber Trip Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Uber trip data and provide meaningful business insights into bookings, revenue, customers, vehicles, and payment methods.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard helps analyze Uber's operational performance by tracking key business metrics such as completed rides, revenue, booking status, vehicle performance, customer trends, and payment methods. The project demonstrates how raw data can be transformed into actionable insights using Power BI.

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- Auto generated the highest overall revenue.
- UPI was the most preferred payment method.
- More than **93K rides** were successfully completed.
- Driver cancellations were higher than customer cancellations.
- Customer ratings remained consistently above **4.4**, indicating high customer satisfaction.
- Monthly revenue remained stable with slight fluctuations throughout the year.
